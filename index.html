BOOM or BUST

If you've played blackjack before, this should be familiar to you.

You have four chips here. Each chip has a value between 1 and 12, but what those values are is completely random and changes every time you play.

Click on the chips to add them to the pot until you reach the goal (also completely random and changing with each play). If the total in the pot equals the goal, you win! If you go over, you lose. No stopping early and hoping the dealer busts (remember, this ain't blackjack!).

Will you hit it big...or will you bust out?

Good Luck!

(Also, just a word of caution: Be careful if you're on mobile. Tapping too fast will zoom in the screen.)
